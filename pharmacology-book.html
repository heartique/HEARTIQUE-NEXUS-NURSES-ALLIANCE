💡 Study Tips

  • 🧠 Use mnemonics and memory aids (like “SLUDGE” for cholinergic effects).
  • 📑 Create a drug classification cheat-sheet to revise quickly.
  • 🗂 Group drugs by function: antibiotics, cardiac meds, CNS, etc.
  • 📌 Practice case-based thinking: “What would I monitor for this drug?”
