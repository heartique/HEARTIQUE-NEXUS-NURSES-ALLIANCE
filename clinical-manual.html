💡 Study Tips

  • 📝 Read and visualize the procedure before trying it practically.
  • 🧼 Learn correct steps for handwashing, IV care, wound dressing, etc.
  • 🎯 Always understand the “why” behind every step — not just the “how.”
  • 🔁 Practice and self-check regularly using simulation or checklists.
